Welcome to my page!

I’m a synthetic and computational biologist with a PhD in Chemical and Biomolecular Engineering and over 14 years of academic and industry experience bridging biotechnology and computational sciences. My hands-on work spans microbial physiology, genetic engineering (CRISPR), and molecular biology (NGS) on the experimental side, and bioinformatics, data science, and software DevOps on the computational side to drive research and innovation in biotech. I’m passionate about leveraging AI/ML to accelerate scientific discovery and development, transforming complex challenges into practical, real-world solutions. A collaborative team player, I’ve led and supported multidisciplinary projects, contributing to multiple publications and patent applications.

This site collects my projects, CV, and occasional writing on computational
biology, synthetic biology, and AI.
